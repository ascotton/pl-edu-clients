District Admin - Person who handles administrative decisions at an organization/district level
IEP Access Contact - This person may provide your IEP system user name/password, and may direct you to IEP training resources.
IT Contact - This person is responsible for troubleshooting IT problems at the school. For your own personal technical support needs, contact PresenceLearning.
Primary Site Contact - This person is the overall administrative head at a location (school) level.
Primary Support Person - Person responsible for helping students to and from therapy sessions
Scheduling Contact - Person who knows bell and holiday schedules and can generally help coordinate things
Site Contact - Person to contact regarding general questions at the organization or location level
Staff (Other) - Use this role if none of the above roles apply
